Cookies Policy.

This policy explains which cookies aionstudio.tech uses, what they are for and how to manage them, in accordance with the GDPR, the ePrivacy Directive and the CNIL guidance (France), as well as the Spanish LSSI-CE (Art. 22.2). This page is also available in español · français.

What is a cookie

A cookie is a small file that a website stores on your device. Some are essential for the site to work; others are used to measure usage or to personalise.

Cookies we use

TypePurposeConsent required?
Technical / essential Basic operation of the site and submission of the contact form No
Analytics Audience measurement without cookies (anonymous and aggregated, no personal data) No

No consent banner. This website is hand-built (HTML/CSS/JS, with no third-party managers or plugins), loads no analytics or trackers (no Google Analytics, no pixels, no Hotjar), embeds no videos or maps, and the form is submitted without setting cookies. Since we use no non-essential cookies, there is nothing to consent to, which is why we display no banner.

What we do not use

We use no tracking, advertising or social-media cookies. There are no third-party trackers or embedded content setting cookies.

How to manage them

Since we use no non-essential cookies, you do not need to configure anything. You can nonetheless set your browser to block or delete cookies (Chrome, Firefox, Safari and Edge all allow this from their privacy settings).

If we were to add a non-essential cookie in future (for example, classic cookie-based analytics or an embedded widget), we would ask you beforehand via a banner allowing you to refuse as easily as to accept, and we would update this page.

More information
